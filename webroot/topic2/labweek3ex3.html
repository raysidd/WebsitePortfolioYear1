Growing Up in London - Rayyan Siddiqui

I grew up in the hometown of London. It is known as the capital of the UK.
Life here has been quite thrilling and unforgettable.


London is a nice place to live in as it is very multicultural.
Furthermore, the city is home to QMUL a university where I currently study my degree at.


London is also home to some fascinating landmarks and attractions which hold a lot of history about the country.
The city has generated a lot of money for the country mainly due to the tourism attractions available here.


One fact about London is that it is actually the smallest city in England.
This is ironic seeing as it's the best place to move to in England.


Another fact about London is that it has more Indian restaurants than Mumbai.
Another fact would be that the infamous London Underground could have been water-based.


London being one of the world's most visited cities, is what makes me proud to be a Londoner.
Thank you for tuning into my description of London.
